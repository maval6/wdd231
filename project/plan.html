Site Plan: Global Gateway Travel Services

Site Name

Global Gateway Travel Services

This name reflects an organization offering international travel support and planning services. It's broad, professional, and easy to remember. A potential domain name could be globalgatewaytravel.com.

Site Purpose

The website provides information and resources for travelers looking to explore global destinations. Services include trip planning, visa guidance, live weather info, destination spotlights, and currency converters. It aims to be a helpful and reliable hub for travelers worldwide.

Scenarios

  • What are the visa requirements for visiting Japan from the USA?
  • Can I see a travel spotlight or blog post about top places to visit in South Africa?

Color Schema

  • Primary Color: #005f73 (Deep Ocean Blue) – Used for headers and buttons
  • Accent Color: #94d2bd (Seafoam Green) – Used for call-to-action buttons, links, and highlights
  • Background: #f1faee – Page background
  • Text: #333333 – Body and paragraph text

Typography

  • Heading Font: "Merriweather", serif – For all headings (h1-h4)
  • Body Font: "Open Sans", sans-serif – For all paragraphs and labels
